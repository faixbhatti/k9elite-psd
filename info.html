WHAT IS K9ELITE?

K9Elite Dog Training is a team of canine behavioral specialist. We specialize in aggression, anxiety, fearbiting, hyperactivity, and many more canine personalities. We take the training to the comfort of your home and we don't leave until the job is done. The reason why our training works so well is because we apply the basic psychological factors that derives from any dogs decedent, the wolf. Our success comes educating our clients. We teach them all the secrets they need to know.

MISSION

Our goal is to keep dogs out of shelters and in happy and safe homes. We can achieve this by educating more people and teaching their pups to respect the home.
